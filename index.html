Spartan Training

Wishes & Regrets

To express regret about the past (wishing a past event was different), we use:

I WISH / IF ONLY + PAST PERFECT

"I wish I had told him sooner."

(Reality: I didn't tell him, and now I regret it.)

"If only we hadn't gone to Jötunheim."

Spartan Training

Past Speculation

To make deductions or speculate about things that already happened, we use Modals in the past:

MODAL + HAVE + PAST PARTICIPLE

Must Have: 99% sure it happened.

"Faye must have known we would face the gods."

Might / Could Have: 50% possibility.

"He might have survived the fall."

Couldn't Have: 99% sure it's impossible.

"They couldn't have defeated Thor alone."
